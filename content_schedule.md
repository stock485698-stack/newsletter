# Newsletter Content Schedule: The AI Investor

This schedule outlines a consistent publishing rhythm and thematic focus for your newsletter, ensuring a steady flow of valuable content for your subscribers.

## Publishing Frequency

*   **Weekly:** Every Tuesday morning (EST)

## Content Pillars

1.  **Feature Article:** In-depth analysis of a key AI/finance topic.
2.  **Market Snapshot:** AI-driven insights into current market trends.
3.  **AI Tool Spotlight:** Review or highlight of a relevant AI tool or platform.
4.  **Community & Updates:** News, Q&A, or announcements (e.g., Premium launch).

## Content Creation Workflow

1.  **Monday:** Research and outline feature article.
2.  **Tuesday:** Draft market snapshot and tool spotlight. Write first draft of issue.
3.  **Wednesday:** Edit, proofread, and finalize content. Prepare for distribution.
4.  **Thursday:** Schedule email for Tuesday delivery. Begin research for next issue.

## Engagement Strategy

*   **Call to Action:** Each issue will include a clear call to action (e.g., "Reply with your questions," "Share with a friend," "Check out our new About page").
*   **Feedback Loop:** Encourage subscribers to respond with topics they'd like to see covered.
*   **Social Sharing:** Provide easy ways for subscribers to share content on social media.
